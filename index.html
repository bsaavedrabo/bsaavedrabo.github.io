Research CV

Belen Saavedra


I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Informatics at Indiana University, working in the ProHealth Lab under the supervision of Patrick Shih. My research focuses on contextual speech recognition systems for atypical speech, particularly in clinical and assistive settings. I earned my B.A. in Computer Science from Berea College in 2024, where I was advised by Dr. Jasmine Jones. I also volunteer with AIEmpower, contributing to research that supports the stuttering community.

I am currently studying the lifecycle of datasets, with an emphasis on how they are created, used and maintained
