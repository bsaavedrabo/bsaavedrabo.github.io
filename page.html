Belen Saavedra

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Informatics at the ProHealth Lab at Indiana University, advised by Patrick Shih. My research investigates speech recognition systems for atypical speech, particularly in clinical and assistive contexts. I am interested in improving model robustness and fairness for underrepresented speech patterns and in designing systems that meaningfully support speech-language pathologists
